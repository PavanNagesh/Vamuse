Instant confirmation of ticket bookings via email or SMS offers users unparalleled convenience and peace of mind. By receiving immediate confirmation of their reservations, users can confidently proceed with their plans, knowing that their tickets are secured as soon as the payment is processed. This eliminates any uncertainty or ambiguity about the status of their booking and allows them to bypass the hassle of waiting in line or dealing with physical tickets. Additionally, instant confirmation provides users with flexibility to manage their bookings effortlessly, whether it's rescheduling or canceling their reservations. Sanitization Stations

In response to the ongoing public health concerns, we've implemented enhanced sanitization measures throughout the park, placing a strong emphasis on the availability and strategic placement of hand sanitization stations. These stations have been strategically positioned at key locations across the park, including entrances, exits, restrooms, food courts, and popular attractions. These stations are easily accessible to visitors without getting disrupted by their park experience.

Health Screenings

In alignment with our commitment to prioritize the health and safety of our visitors, we've implemented comprehensive health screenings at entry points throughout the park. These screenings include temperature checks to identify individuals who may have a fever, a common symptom of many contagious illnesses. These measures are crucial in ensuring the well-being of everyone within the park premises.

Staff Training

Visitors can rest assured that our park staff undergo rigorous training in proper hygiene practices to ensure the safety and well-being of all guests. From frontline attendants to behind-the-scenes personnel, every team member is equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to maintain high standards of cleanliness and sanitation throughout the park. Furthermore, our staff are provided with appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE).

Online Booking Benefits

We highly encourage visitors to take advantage of our convenient online booking system when purchasing tickets for their park visit. Not only does online booking provide a seamless and efficient experience, but it also helps minimize physical contact and reduce waiting times at the entrance, allowing guests to make the most of their time in the park. By booking online, visitors can skip the lines at the ticket counters and gain direct entry upon arrival, streamlining their entry process and enhancing overall convenience

Contactless Payment

In our commitment to providing a safe and convenient experience for all visitors, we strongly encourage the use of contactless payment options for all transactions within the park. By utilizing contactless payment methods such as mobile payments, tap-to-pay cards, or wearable technology, guests can minimize the handling of cash and cards, reducing potential points of contact and enhancing overall hygiene. Contactless payment offers several benefits, including increased speed and efficiency at transaction points
